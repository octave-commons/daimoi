## What daimoi are here

Daimoi are treated as:
- packets with owners
- movers in a field
- carriers of intent distributions
- subjects of measurement and governance

## What this repo is not

Not yet a production package.
Not yet the full extracted code from Part64.
Not yet the full visual language or panel system.

It is the place where the semantics become readable enough to rebuild without reopening the whole vault.
